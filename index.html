About Stocks

Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you're buying a share of that company's future earnings. The stock market allows investors to buy and sell these shares, helping companies raise capital and investors grow wealth.

Stock prices fluctuate based on supply and demand, company performance, global events, and market sentiment. Staying informed helps investors make better decisions.
